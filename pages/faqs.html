Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How often should I clean my clothing iron?

We recommend cleaning your clothing iron after every few uses. Regular cleaning helps maintain its performance and prevents build-up of residue.

2. Can I use tap water in my iron?

Yes, you can use tap water in your iron. However, if your tap water is hard, it is advisable to use distilled water to prevent mineral build-up.

3. What should I do if my iron is not heating up?

If your iron is not heating up, check if it is properly plugged in and the temperature setting is correct. If the problem persists, refer to the troubleshooting section in the user manual or contact our customer support.

4. How can I remove scorch marks from my iron?

To remove scorch marks, allow the iron to cool down, then clean the soleplate with a mixture of baking soda and water. Avoid using abrasive cleaners that could damage the soleplate.
